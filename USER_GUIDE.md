## ğŸ’¡ Best Practices for Maximum Utility

1. **Be Honest with Focus Ratings:** When logging an activity, rate your focus accurately (1-5). Logging 4 hours of distracted study (Focus: 2) is mathematically worse than 2 hours of deep work (Focus: 5). The app's algorithms rely on this honesty.
2. **End-of-Day Ritual:** Make it a hard rule to fill out the **Diary** every single evening. The 2 minutes it takes to log your Energy and Mood is the bedrock for the app's advanced *Burnout* and *Momentum* analytics.
3. **Don't Over-Track:** Start with 3-5 core activities. Avoid the temptation to track every second of your day (e.g., eating, showering). Only track activities where you have *targets* to hit or *improvements* to measure (e.g., Study subjects, Gym, Deep reading).
4. **Use Tags in Tests:** Prefix your test topics consistently (e.g., `Polity-FundamentalRights`, `Economy-Macro`) so that trends naturally group your weaker areas in the Analytics view.
5. **Back up regularly:** Go to the Settings tab regularly and export your data as a JSON file. Because the app is 100% offline and local, you control your data security.
